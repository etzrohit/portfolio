How I work

Three things that shape every project

01

I ship in code, not just Figma

AI-paired tooling means I can take a design straight into a real codebase, work alongside engineers, and land a reviewed, merged PR myself.

02

I make bets, and I reverse them

I'll commit to a direction, ship it, and if it's wrong — like a technical approach that stops paying off — I'll cut it and re-execute rather than defend the sunk cost.

03

I design for the stakes, not just the screen

When the product is infrastructure other businesses run on, a confusing screen isn't a cosmetic problem — it's downstream risk. That framing drives every prioritization call.
