Problem Statement

Design a mobile app called Wanderlust that helps users organize travel itineraries and discover new destinations efficiently. The project was completed as part of an assignment within a two-day deadline.

Introduction

Wanderlust simplifies travel planning by offering users a seamless experience to create itineraries, discover attractions, and manage expenses. The app was designed to cater to modern travelers seeking personalized and intuitive travel tools.

Core Problem

Travelers face challenges in organizing itineraries, discovering new places, and managing travel expenses. This process often becomes fragmented and time-consuming, especially without the right tools to streamline these tasks.

Core user/s

Wanderlust is designed for Millennial travelers (ages 18-35), including solo travelers and adventure seekers. These tech-savvy users prioritize efficient planning, personalized recommendations, and real-time travel assistance.

User Story

Sarah is a young professional who loves traveling during vacations and exploring new destinations. She enjoys immersing herself in different cultures, trying local cuisine, and visiting famous landmarks. Sarah has a busy schedule and relies on mobile apps to plan her trips efficiently.

Sarah’s Goals:

  • Organize trip details seamlessly.
  • Discover new destinations with personalized recommendations.
  • Access real-time support during trips, such as maps and nearby attractions.
  • Read travel content for inspiration.
  • Prefer a user-friendly and intuitive interface.

Challenges:

  • Sarah often struggles with information overload and finding concise, reliable travel information.
  • She has limited time to plan trips and needs a tool that streamlines the process.

Solution Overview

Travelers like Sarah struggle to efficiently plan their trips, discover new destinations, and access relevant information while on the go. Existing travel apps often lack a comprehensive and user-friendly experience, making it challenging to organize itineraries, find personalized recommendations, and receive on-the-go assistance, resulting in a fragmented and time-consuming travel planning process.

Wanderlust addresses these pain points by offering:

  • Simplified itinerary management.
  • Personalized destination recommendations.
  • On-the-go assistance for travel logistics and activities.
  • Expense tracking
  • A clean, intuitive interface with a minimal learning curve.

Metrics for Success

  • Itinerary Completion Rate: The number of users who complete trip planning using the app.
  • Active Users: Measuring how frequently users engage with the app during their trips.
  • Feature Adoption: The use of AI recommendations and collaborative planning features.

Designs

Onboarding

  • Simple and informative, guiding users through the app’s core functions.
  • Screenshots and instructions are included to help first-time users.

Log in/Sign up

  • Supports email and social login with OTP verification.
  • Users provide names and phone numbers to enable group travel coordination.
    Why phone number is needed?
    • Simplified Group Creation: By syncing contacts via the mobile number, users can easily invite friends and family to join travel groups. This eliminates extra steps, making collaboration and coordination smoother.
    • Timely Notifications: With the mobile number, the app can send relevant updates, reminders, and notifications, keeping users informed of any itinerary changes or important trip details.

Navigation

  • Key sections include Home, AI bot, Trips, and Profile.
  • Home: Destination search, nearby suggestions, and blog content.
    More details
    • The home screen includes a fixed search bar for users to easily find any destination. It also suggests nearby destinations based on the user's location for quick exploration.
    • A prominent "Create Trip" button is placed below the search bar, followed by interest-based categories. This section adapts dynamically to the user's preferences, showing relevant activities and attractions based on their current or ongoing trip.
    • A featured blog section allows users like Sarah to share travel experiences, promote itineraries, and even offer local guide services, with potential for further business opportunities.
  • AI bot: Recommends activities and answers travel-related questions.
  • Trips: Allows users to create and manage travel itineraries.
  • Profile: Stores invoices, support options, and settings.

Given Wanderlust's early startup stage, including hotel, cab, and flight bookings is impractical. Users typically prefer booking these services separately to secure better prices. By concentrating on core features like itinerary planning, destination discovery, and expense management, Wanderlust can offer a unique value proposition without competing with established booking platforms. Future integration of booking features can be explored as the app develops.

Trip Creation

  • Easy-to-use trip creation process with customizable itinerary features.

Ongoing Trip Management

  • Users can access four key options in the ongoing trip section: Overview, Explore, Itinerary, and Expenses, enhancing their travel experience.
  • Users can manage trip details, upload attachments, and create to-do lists.
  • Explore: Personalized suggestions for activities and attractions.
  • Expense Management: Simplified cost-splitting feature for group trips.
  1. Overview
    • The Overview section allows users to manage their group, including adding members and editing trip details.
    • Users can upload important documents (e.g., hotel bookings, tickets) and sync messages or emails to keep everything in one place.
    • A comprehensive to-do list helps users avoid forgetting tasks, allowing them to assign items to specific group members.
    • All itinerary locations are consolidated in one view, eliminating the need to switch between dates.
  1. Itinerary
    • Users have full control in the Itinerary section, where they can add or remove places and customize their plans.
    • Navigation between days is seamless, with easy access to Google Maps for directions.
  1. Explore
    • The Explore feature helps users discover the best places and activities, utilizing category filters for tailored recommendations. It includes a video section for visual guidance and a comprehensive range of resources, such as blogs, top recommendations, and local insights.
    • Inner pages for blogs, activities, and attractions provide essential information (pricing, timings, etc.) and include FAQs and user reviews for informed decision-making.
  1. Expense
    • Group members can add and split expenses easily, with the current setup allowing one person to cover costs, which are then divided equally among the group. While additional expense-splitting solutions were considered, time constraints limited the exploration of these options.
